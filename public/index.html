Instructions

1. Press 'Control arm'.

2. Enter your password.

3. Use key controls and mouse:

  • up = arm forward
  • down = arm backwards
  • left = rotate arm left
  • right = rotate arm right
  • w = wrist up
  • s = wrist down

Mouse controls:

Drag the white dot and move your mouse up, down and left and right. Release or bring back to center to stop moving.

  • mouse left = rotate arm left
  • mouse right = rotate arm right
  • mouse up = arm forwards
  • mouse down = arm backwards

  • shift + mouse left = rotate wrist left
  • shift + mouse right = rotate arm right
  • shift + mouse up = arm up
  • shift + mouse down = arm down
